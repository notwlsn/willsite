Career

I haven't been in industry long, but I'm already restless with its state. It's my
personal opinion that security is not taken nearly seriously enough, or if it
is, it's treated as a chore. I believe our systems are far gone. So far, my
action has been to raise awareness in my immediate surroundings and show my
co-workers why they need to do things they consider 'inconvenient'.
